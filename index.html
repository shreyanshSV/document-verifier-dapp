Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I verify my birth certificate?

Go to the Document Verification section, select "Birth Certificate" from the dropdown, enter your certificate number, and upload the document.

2. What documents are supported?

We support birth certificates, educational certificates, property documents, and identity cards issued by Jharkhand government.

3. How long does verification take?

Most verifications are completed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once the process is complete.
